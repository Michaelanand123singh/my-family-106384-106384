About My Family

My Family is a non-profit organization dedicated to the rescue, rehabilitation, and rehoming of abandoned and neglected animals. We believe that every animal deserves a loving home and a chance at a happy life.

Our team of dedicated volunteers and staff work tirelessly to provide the best possible care for the animals in our charge. We rely on the generosity of our supporters to continue our mission.
